## 👨‍🔬 About Me

<p align="justify">
I am a <b>Postdoctoral Researcher</b> in the Microbial Ecology and Biotechnology (MEB) Section, Department of Plant and Environmental Sciences (PLEN), at the <b>University of Copenhagen (UCPH), Denmark</b>.
My research lies at the intersection of <b>Artificial Intelligence and Biological Sciences</b>. Currently, I apply <b>AI-driven bioinformatics</b> to investigate and engineer <b>PFAS-degrading enzymes</b> for sustainable environmental solutions.
Previously, I completed my PhD in Bioinformatics (AI in Biology) at Jamia Millia Islamia, India, focusing on AI and computational drug discovery for lung cancer therapeutics.
</p>
